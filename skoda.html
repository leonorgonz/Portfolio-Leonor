Škoda

A rebranding project developed in 2022, anticipating Škoda’s official redesign in 2023 to reflect its transition toward electric mobility and sustainability. The project aimed to modernize the brand while preserving its heritage, translating Škoda’s values of design, function, and innovation into a contemporary visual identity.


Škoda has evolved from bicycles and motorcycles to cars that are often ahead of their time, and this project reinforces its vision of a sustainable future while communicating progress, environmental responsibility, and technological innovation.

Briefing

Redesign Škoda’s identity to modernize the brand, maintain its heritage, and ensure a consistent visual language across all applications, reflecting its leadership in electric mobility.

Logo construction

The new logo is based on a modified Secrets Stencil typeface. A symbolic arrow above the “S” represents progress and direction. The system includes a green-centered color palette and a modern typographic system, ensuring clarity and consistency across print and digital media.

Visual Identity

The identity was applied across stationery, posters, web, and merchandising, creating a cohesive system. Each element works together to maintain a recognizable, consistent, and modern brand that communicates innovation, sustainability, and progress.
